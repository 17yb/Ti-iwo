# Syntax Examples

## Question Word
Hina'o - Where?

## Verb
Tahi - To See

### Verb Stem
Tahi - To See

### Imperative
I vonomia! - Leave/Go away!

### Negative
Ki tahikano - He does not see

### Passive
To uli tahi - I am seen

### Reflective
Uve tahi todoto to - I see myself

### Gerundives
Touro eu tahi - I must/need to see

### Switch Reference
Tahi to Kayo, hiaena'i amaho - I am Caio, I like food
